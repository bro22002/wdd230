Audience

The target audience will be business owners, business organizations, entrepreneurs, and those in high positions who want to promote their business and increase growth as well as support the community by providing employment for the youth. The target audience will typically fall between the ages of 35 to 75 years. The audience will be business personnel with high income. They will be living in the Central Region of Ghana. Most visitors will be visiting our websites via mobile devices. We will make it possible for them to access our sites via computer too. The Chamber will highlight the positive aspects of living in the community to promote tourism and cultural-based member businesses, increase tourism revenue for the city, and encourage businesses and the workforce they employ to relocate to the area.
