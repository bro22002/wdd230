The Cape Coast Chamber of Commerce (CCC) is an association of business operators, firms, and industries with interests spanning every sector of private enterprise in the Central Region, Cape Coast, Ghana. As an advocacy organization, the Cape Coast Chamber of Commerce was established with the prime objective of promoting and protecting commercial and industrial interests in the country, especially in the Central Region of Ghana.

The Chamber represents the voice of the business community in the Central Region of Ghana with a total commitment to playing a leadership role in propelling Ghana's economy to higher heights. As an autonomous business support organization, we serve as a link between established businesses, the emerging sector, and government at various levels while offering our members a myriad of business support services.

To this end, the Cape Coast Chamber has and will continue to influence government policies and programs, which will inure to the benefit of the private sector while promoting the overall development of the Ghanaian economy. Our vision is to provide leadership for the growth and prosperity of businesses in Ghana.
